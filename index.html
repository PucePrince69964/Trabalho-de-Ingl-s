HISTORY

The protagonist is a 15-year-old teenager who enters Hogwarts late, entering his fifth year. He competes against Ranrock, a dark goblin, to obtain the repository of ancient magic. To do so, he goes through four trials, each of which gives him an artifact containing memories that tell the story of a witch named Isidora Morganach, who, like the protagonist, is able to see traces of ancient magic. With this, Isidora has access to a magic that removes people's emotions. She uses this magic on her family and students at Hogwarts and stores this magic in repositories. Four teachers who also have the ability to see this magic, called "guardians," use the curse of death, Avada Kedavra, on Isidora to stop her. The four guardians use a magic to erase memories, Obliviate, on the only being who knows of Isidora's activities, which is an goblin named Bragbor. However, Bragbor writes journals with all the information he knows about what happened, and thanks to them, Ranrock finds out about the repositories and is able to find them. Ranrock has all the repositories and is looking for the last one. Upon discovering the location of the last repository, the protagonist and his mentor, Professor Fig, both go after it before Ranrock can reach them. Before they go, the guardians warn the protagonist that he will be tempted to unleash and use the repository's ancient magic, just as Isidora was years before. Upon reaching the protagonist and his mentor, Ranrock reveals that he possesses the wand of Miriam, Professor Fig's wife and a witch who discovers Ronrock's activities but is killed by him when she tries to stop him. Soon the battle between them begins. In the middle of the battle, Ranrock uses magic to counter the protagonist's attack and ends up hitting the repository. As a result, the repository is destroyed and Ranrock absorbs its power, turning into a dragon.

ENDINGS

1° -The game has three possible endings, which happen according to the player's choices.

2° -In the first ending, still during the battle against Ranrock, the protagonist gives in to temptation and absorbs the ancestral magic from the repository. He uses this magic to kill Ranrock and, after that, he absorbs all the rest of the ancestral magic. The protagonist, in the end, becomes a dark witch and a possible great threat to the witch world.

3° -In the second ending, also during the battle, the protagonist manages to resist the temptation and chooses to keep the existence of the ancestral magic a secret. After killing Ranrock, the protagonist, with the help of the wounded Professor Fig, manages to seal the ancestral magic again. The professor, in the end, is unable to resist and dies before the protagonist. The protagonist then becomes the new guardian of the ancient magic.

HOGWARTS HOUSES

Upon arrival at Hogwarts, the protagonist is selected for his house by the Sorting Hat. The houses at Hogwarts that he can be selected to are four, being Gryffindor, Slytherin, Hufflepuff and Ravenclaw. You can choose which house your character goes to just by selecting the alternative that contains the main characteristic of the desired house. Gryffindor's main characteristic is boldness, Ravenclaw's is curiosity, Hufflepuff's is loyalty, and Slytherin's is ambition.

AVAILABLE SPELLS

    The spells available for use in the game are divided into colors, which are yellow, purple, red and gray. The spells that are classified in yellow are control spells, those in red are damage spells, those in purple are strength spells, and those in gray are ancestral spells. The available spells include:

  • Lumos: It illuminates the environment.
  • Accio: It summons an object nearby.
  • Alohomora: It opens locks.
  • Aberto: It is a weaker and archaic version of Alohomora.
  • Confrigo: It casts fireballs at enemies.
  • Descendo: It casts objects or individuals on the ground.
  • Incendio: It casts flames on enemies.
  • Petrificus Totalus: It momentarily paralyzes the opponent.
  • Stupefy: It knocks down an enemy.
  • Protego: It blocks physical and magic attacks.
  • Revelio: It reveals hidden objects, messages and paths.
  • Leviosa: It makes objects and individuals levitate.
  • Levioso: It is a more archaic version of Leviosa, and it has a shorter duration.
  • Oppugno: It allows you to attack an enemy with objects.
  • Invisible: It allows you to remain invisible for a certain amount of time.
  • Diffindo: It allows you to cut anything, especially objects.
  • Expelliarmus: It disarms your opponent.
  • Glacius: It freezes enemies.
  • Reparo: It repairs any object.
  • Aguamenti: It conjures up water.
  • Arresto Momentum: It allows you to slow down the speed in which something falls.
  • Finite: It reverses magical effects.
  • Reducto: It lets you blow up something.
  • Imperio: It allows you to control the enemy's mind.
  • Crucio: It allows you to torture your enemy.
  • Avada Kedavra: It allows you to instantly kill your enemy.
